## What is CyberVault?

CyberVault is a privacy-first desktop file vault. It lets you store your most
sensitive files behind a locally-encrypted, password-protected vault wrapped in
a sleek cyberpunk interface.

### Highlights

- 🔐 **Local encryption** — your files never leave your machine in plain form.
- 🗂️ **Categories & organization** — keep everything tidy and searchable.
- 🖼️ **Built-in viewer** — preview images and files without exposing them.
- 📱 **Phone access** — securely reach your vault from another device.
- 🧪 **Integrity checks** — verify your data hasn't been tampered with.
- 🎨 **Themeable neon UI** — multiple cyberpunk color themes.

> ⚠️ **Important:** Your password is never stored. There is no master key and no
> backdoor. If you forget your password, your data is gone for good. Write it
> down somewhere safe.
